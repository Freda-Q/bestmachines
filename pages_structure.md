# Multi-Page Website Structure for Best Machines

## Overview

This document outlines the complete information architecture and visual design for five independent pages, maintaining the international engineering aesthetic established in the homepage.

---

## 1. Products Page

**URL:** `/products`

**Purpose:** Showcase the complete injection molding machine product lineup with detailed specifications and technical capabilities.

**Content Structure:**

- **Hero Section:** Bold headline "Advanced Injection Molding Solutions" with a high-quality machine image or 3D render, emphasizing precision and reliability.
- **Product Categories:** Grid layout displaying machine types (e.g., Hydraulic Series, Electric Series, Hybrid Series) with category descriptions and key specifications.
- **Product Cards:** Each product card includes machine model, key specs (clamping force, injection volume, cycle time), application suitability, and "View Details" CTA.
- **Comparison Table:** Side-by-side comparison of major models highlighting differences in capacity, speed, and energy efficiency.
- **Technical Specifications Section:** Detailed breakdown of standard features, optional modules, and customization options.
- **CTA Section:** "Request a Quote" and "Schedule a Demo" buttons.

**Visual Assets Needed:**
- Hero background image (industrial machine facility or precision manufacturing)
- 5–8 product category images (one per machine type)
- Technical specification diagrams or infographics
- Comparison chart visual

## 2. Applications Page

**URL:** `/applications`

**Purpose:** Demonstrate real-world use cases and industry-specific applications with visual examples and case study teasers.

**Content Structure:**

- **Hero Section:** "Industries We Serve" with a dynamic visual showing multiple application sectors.
- **Application Categories Grid:** 6–8 major industries (Packaging, Consumer Goods, Medical, Automotive, Construction, Electronics, etc.) displayed as cards with industry image, description, and typical product examples.
- **Deep Dive Sections:** For each major application, include:
  - Industry overview and market trends
  - Specific challenges solved by Best Machines
  - Machine recommendations and configurations
  - Customer success metrics or testimonials
- **Case Study Carousel:** Featured customer case studies with project photos, results, and ROI highlights.
- **CTA Section:** "Explore Our Solutions" and "Get Industry-Specific Recommendations."

**Visual Assets Needed:**
- Hero image (manufacturing diversity or global industry)
- 6–8 application category images (high-quality, industry-specific)
- 3–5 case study photos (factory floor, finished products, customer facilities)
- Industry icons or badges

## 3. Capabilities Page

**URL:** `/capabilities`

**Purpose:** Highlight technical capabilities, manufacturing standards, and competitive advantages.

**Content Structure:**

- **Hero Section:** "Why Choose Best Machines" with a tagline emphasizing precision, reliability, and innovation.
- **Core Capabilities Matrix:** Visual breakdown of key strengths:
  - Precision & Accuracy (tolerances, repeatability)
  - Speed & Efficiency (cycle times, energy consumption)
  - Flexibility & Customization (modular design, quick changeover)
  - Quality & Compliance (certifications, standards)
  - Support & Service (technical support, spare parts availability)
- **Technology Highlights:** Sections on proprietary technology, automation features, IoT connectivity, and sustainability initiatives.
- **Certifications & Standards:** Display ISO, CE, and industry-specific certifications with brief explanations.
- **Performance Benchmarks:** Charts or infographics comparing Best Machines against industry standards.
- **Innovation Timeline:** Visual representation of R&D milestones and product evolution.
- **CTA Section:** "Discover Advanced Features" and "Compare with Competitors."

**Visual Assets Needed:**
- Hero background (precision manufacturing or technology lab)
- Capability icons or badges (6–8 icons)
- Performance comparison charts
- Certification logos
- Innovation timeline graphics

## 4. Company Page

**URL:** `/company`

**Purpose:** Build trust and credibility by sharing company history, values, team, and global presence.

**Content Structure:**

- **Hero Section:** "About Best Machines" with company tagline and a professional company image or facility photo.
- **Company Overview:** Brief history, founding story, and mission statement.
- **Key Milestones:** Timeline of major achievements, product launches, and market expansions.
- **Values & Vision:** Visual representation of core company values (Innovation, Quality, Customer Focus, Sustainability).

- **Team Highlights:** Leadership team profiles with photos, titles, and brief bios. Optionally include team culture photos.
- **Sustainability & CSR:** Initiatives related to environmental responsibility, community engagement, and industry leadership.
- **Awards & Recognition:** Industry awards, certifications, and media mentions.
- **CTA Section:** "Join Our Team" (careers link) and "Partner With Us."

**Visual Assets Needed:**
- Hero company image (facility, team, or corporate headquarters)
- Company timeline graphics
- Global map with location markers
- Team member photos
- Award/certification logos
- Sustainability infographics

## 5. Contact Page

**URL:** `/contact`

**Purpose:** Provide multiple channels for customer inquiries, support, and business development.

**Content Structure:**

- **Hero Section:** "Get in Touch" with a professional, inviting visual.
- **Contact Methods Grid:** Display multiple contact options:
  - Sales inquiries (email, phone, form)
  - Technical support (hotline, ticketing system)
  - General inquiries (contact form)
  - Partnerships (dedicated contact)
- **Contact Form:** Comprehensive form with fields for:
  - Name, email, phone, company
  - Inquiry type (Sales, Support, Partnership, Other)
  - Machine type of interest (dropdown)
  - Message/details
  - Preferred contact method and timeframe
  - Checkbox for newsletter subscription
- **Office Locations:** Cards for each regional office with address, phone, email, and hours.
- **Embedded Map:** Interactive map showing all office locations.
- **FAQ Section:** Quick answers to common questions about ordering, support, and customization.
- **Response Time Commitment:** Clear messaging about expected response times.

**Visual Assets Needed:**
- Hero background (professional office or customer service)
- Office location photos (optional)
- Contact method icons
- Map integration (Google Maps or similar)

## Design Consistency Guidelines

**Typography:** Maintain the same font hierarchy as the homepage (bold display fonts for headings, clean sans-serif for body text).

**Color Palette:** Consistent use of primary brand blue, accent orange, neutral grays, and white space.

**Layout:** All pages follow a modular section-based layout with consistent spacing, padding, and breakpoints for responsive design.

**Navigation:** Fixed or sticky header with navigation menu linking to all five pages plus homepage. Mobile-friendly hamburger menu.

**CTAs:** Consistent button styles and colors across all pages. Primary CTAs use brand blue; secondary CTAs use outlined style.

**Images:** All images maintain consistent aspect ratios, quality standards, and professional aesthetic aligned with the engineering/manufacturing industry.

**Responsiveness:** All pages are fully responsive, tested on mobile (320px), tablet (768px), and desktop (1024px+) viewports.
